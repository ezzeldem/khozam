Go Heliopolis

A WHOLE NEW PERSPECTIVE

The First Compound in Heliopolis

Go Heliopolis is the first compound in Heliopolis, Located in Nozha Street
Go Heliopolis defines a new concept of luxurious and elegant apartment living, by developing a gated community compound with all its luxurious facilities in the middle of the city.

Go Heliopolis promises an exciting, multi-functional, prime, residential, commercial and well-integrated community set on a land area of 18,000 sqm. Offering a wide variety of residential types ranging from 70 SQM to 185 SQM apartments.
With nearly 800 units, spread over 5 TOWERS, set in a spectacular compound.
Go Heliopolis offers luxurious facilities including wide variety of Office spaces, clinics, banks, pharmacy and supermarket. With amenities and services of housekeeping, parking, security, kids area, rooftop, gym, shopping, restaurants, and cafes. Options within the surrounding neighborhood; coloring the living experience using utmost convenience.

Our project is developed to ensure maximum level of comfort, leisure and ease of life for our residents. In Go Heliopolis we build a dream come home with a lifestyle.
